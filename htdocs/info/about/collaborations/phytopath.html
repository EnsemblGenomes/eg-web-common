PhytoPath

PhytoPath integrates genome-scale data from plant pathogen species with literature-curated information about the phenotypes of host infection. Using the Ensembl browser, it provides access to complete genome assembly and gene models of priority crop and model fungal, oomycete and bacterial phytopathogens. PhytoPath also links genes to disease progression using data from the curated PHI-base resource.
