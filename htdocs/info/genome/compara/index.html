Comparative Genomics

Ensembl Genomes provides cross-genome resources and analyses at both the sequence level and the gene level, using the Ensembl Compara platform. Available analyses include:

  • Peptide comparative genomics providing gene trees and orthology information
    • Statistics on protein trees
  • Whole genome alignments between selected genomes
    • Available alignments
  • Syntenies calculated from genome or peptide alignments
  • Gene families constructed from classification of proteins
